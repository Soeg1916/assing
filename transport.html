Transport in Dessie

Transport

Getting To and Around Dessie

As a key transportation hub in northeastern Ethiopia, Dessie is well-connected to other major cities and regions.

Bus Services

Regular bus services connect Dessie to Addis Ababa, Bahir Dar, and other major cities. The main bus terminal is centrally located and offers various classes of service from basic to luxury coaches.

Road Network

Dessie sits on Ethiopia's main north-south highway, providing good road connections. Recent infrastructure improvements have enhanced the quality of roads leading to and from the city.

Local Transport

Within the city, minibuses (bajaj) and taxis provide affordable transportation. The compact nature of the city center also makes walking a viable option for exploring many attractions.

Motorcycle Taxis

For quick and convenient travel, motorcycle taxis are popular among locals for navigating the city's sometimes steep streets and reaching areas inaccessible to larger vehicles.
