Education in Dessie

Education

Educational Institutions

Dessie has established itself as an educational center in northeastern Ethiopia, with institutions ranging from primary schools to university level.

Wollo University

Founded in 2006, Wollo University is the crown jewel of education in Dessie. The university offers diverse programs across multiple disciplines, including engineering, medicine, agriculture, and social sciences. It attracts students from across Ethiopia and contributes significantly to the city's vibrant atmosphere.

Technical and Vocational Schools

Dessie hosts several technical and vocational training institutions that equip students with practical skills in fields such as construction, automotive technology, textiles, and information technology.

Secondary and Primary Education

The city boasts numerous public and private schools providing quality education at primary and secondary levels. Many of these institutions have long histories and have produced notable alumni who have contributed to Ethiopia's development.
